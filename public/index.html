Los Angeles Bike Share Data Analysis

Trends in Dataset

Duration of Ride (s) vs. Time (weeks) by Passholder Type
This chart, broken down into weeks in the dataset, shows that the duration of rides varies amongst passholder types. The Walk-Up passholders have the longest rides and the most variation in their ride duration, while the monthly and flex passholders spend less time on average on their rides. In addition, the monthly passholders have less variation in their ride duration on average compared to flex passholders.
Duration of Ride (s) vs. Time (weeks) by Trip Route Category
This chart, broken down into weeks in the dataset, shows that the round trips are longer than the one-way trips, which is to be expected. What is interesting about this graph is the fact that there is significantly more variation in the round trips than there is in the one-way trips.
Average Duration of Ride (s) vs. Bike ID
This chart demonstrates that there is not a significant difference in the average durations of rides by Bike ID. This was an interesting point to investigate because if certain bikes were ridden for longer durations, resources may be better invested in maintaining those bikes.
Average Distance of Ride (km) vs. Bike ID
This chart demonstrates that there is not a significant difference in the average distance of rides by Bike ID. This was an interesting point to investigate because if certain bikes were ridden for longer distances, resources may be better invested in maintaining those bikes.
